Sustainability

Include: 1. what platform and tools you used (GitHub Pages, plain HTML/CSS, or Jekyll) 2. what files are essential to the site functioning 3. what data, media, or dependencies a future user would need 4. one sentence on what would be required to maintain or expand the site This page can be brief.
